# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 26, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to 30 Day Me ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web application (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**

- Full name
- Email address
- Username and display name
- Profile picture/avatar

**Challenge and Progress Data:**

- Challenge titles and descriptions
- Personal goals ("wishes") and daily actions
- Challenge start and end dates
- Daily progress completion status

**Authentication Information (Email Sign-Up):**

If you create an account using an email and password, we store your email address and a hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.

**Support and Communication:**

If you contact us directly (for example, by email), we collect your email address and any other information you choose to provide in your message so we can respond.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Session and Device Information:**

- IP address
- User agent (browser type, device type, operating system)
- Session tokens and authentication data
- Timestamps of account creation and updates

**Usage Metrics:**

- Number of completed challenge days
- Performance statistics (used for percentile rankings)

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Authentication

If you choose to sign in using Google or Apple, we receive:

- Your name
- Email address
- Profile picture (if available)

We do not receive or store your password from these providers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service:** Create and manage your account, track your challenges and daily progress, and display your achievements
- **Calculate Performance Metrics:** Generate percentile rankings to show how your progress compares to other users
- **Authenticate Users:** Verify your identity and maintain secure sessions
- **Store Your Content:** Save your challenges and progress data
- **Improve the Service:** Understand usage patterns to enhance functionality

We do not use your personal information for targeted advertising or track you across other apps and websites for advertising purposes. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or marketing trackers.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

- **Database:** Your account information, challenges, and progress data are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon (a cloud database provider)
- **Mobile Device:** Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain or Android Keystore)

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

- Secure session tokens with expiration
- HTTPS encryption for data in transit
- HTTP-only cookies with same-site protection in production
- Platform-native secure storage for mobile authentication tokens
- Foreign key constraints and cascade deletes for data integrity

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** With third-party services that help us operate the Service (as listed in Section 5)
- **Aggregated Data:** We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics (e.g., average completion rates) that do not identify individual users
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, regulation, or legal process
- **Safety:** To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or others

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** We retain your account information and challenge data for as long as your account is active
- **Session Data:** Session tokens expire based on authentication settings and are refreshed as needed
- **Deleted Content:** When you delete challenges or progress entries, they are permanently removed from our systems
- **Account Deletion:** When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted through cascade deletion
